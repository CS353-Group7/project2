Who are EduSign?

We are a group of Third Year Computer Science and Software Engineering students studying at Maynooth University.

As part of one of our modules, we were required to make a web application that was assisted technology.

That is how EduSign was born. Our aim is to help children learn in a friendly environment, but most importantly, in a fun environment!

We understand the difficulty that comes with learning, and how everyone is different, so not all learning techniques suit everyone.

Our aim is to make this website a fun environment that allows its users to leave their worries behind and just enjoy themselves.

For the wonderful parents we want you to feel comfort in knowing your child is having fun but also gaining knowledge.
